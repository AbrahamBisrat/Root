CBOE Volatility Index (VIX) - 30 Day Simulation

The "Fear Gauge"
The VIX measures the market's expectation of 30-day forward-looking volatility derived from S&P 500 index options. High values typically coincide with market stress.
Mean Reversion
Unlike stock prices, the VIX is highly mean-reverting. It tends to spike during crises and settle back to a baseline range of 12-20 during bull markets.
